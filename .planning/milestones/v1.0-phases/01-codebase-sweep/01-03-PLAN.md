<objective>
Remove the Op::Description plugin registrations from DeepCWrapper and DeepCMWrapper so
those abstract base classes are no longer selectable in Nuke's node menu. Also remove the
associated Class() methods that returned d.name (which would become dangling references),
and clean up two known dead-code artifacts in DeepCWrapper.cpp: the commented-out pointer
increments and switch-statement undefined-behavior falls.

Purpose: Artists can currently instantiate DeepCWrapper and DeepCMWrapper as nodes.
These base classes have no meaningful operation — they apply a gain of 1.0 and are
explicitly documented as "should never be used directly." Removing their registrations
makes the plugin suite cleaner. The switch UB fixes prevent potential compiler warnings
under strict builds.

Output: Four patched files (two .cpp, two .h) with registrations and Class() removed,
dead comments deleted, and switch defaults added.
</objective>
